Before you join

Questions people actually ask

Is this a course or a community?
It's a community first. There's no finish line, no certificate hunt. People stay because the room keeps being useful — new sessions, new prompts, new conversations — month after month.
What happens if I can't make a live session?
Every session is recorded and added to the resource library, so you never lose access to what was covered. The community conversation also keeps going outside of live sessions.
Will the price go up later?
For new members, yes — the founding rate is intentionally limited. But once you join at £5/month, that rate is locked in for as long as you remain a member, even if new members later join at a higher rate.
Can I leave if it's not for me?
Yes, any time. There's no contract and no penalty. If you leave, your seat simply becomes available for someone else who's ready for it.
Do I need to already be confident with AI?
No. Members range from people who've never opened an AI tool to people using it daily. Sessions are built to meet people wherever they currently are.
